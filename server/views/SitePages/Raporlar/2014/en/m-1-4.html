Ziraat Finance Group

Ziraat Bank possesses an extensive portfolio of domestic and international subsidiaries and equity stakes that are active in the areas of banking, insurance, private pensions, investment banking, portfolio management, leasing, and financial service technologies.

Considered as a whole, the individual members of the Ziraat Finance Group make up an extensive and global structure that makes it possible for the Ziraat brand’s sustainability performance to be achieved and shared at the broadest scale. This in turn defines the outermost boundaries of the domain of the bank’s influence.2

2) Information about the Ziraat Bank subsidiaries whose results are included in its consolidated financial statements may be found on page 220 of the Bank’s 2014 annual report.

G4-6, G4-8, G4-17

Abroad…
With branches, subsidiaries, and representatives at 85 locations in 16 different countries, Ziraat Bank continues to strengthen its position as the Turkish bank whose direct global reach is more extensive than that of any other bank in Turkey.

Consistent with Ziraat Bank’s international banking strategy and vision, a global array of service points is steadily increasing the bank’s effectiveness in the countries in which it operates.

By contributing to the expansion of commercial and economic relationships between Turkey and the sixteen countries in which it has a presence, Ziraat Bank plays an increasingly greater role in and commands an increasingly bigger share of Turkey’s foreign trade.

Providing fast, state-of-the-art, and superior-quality service to Turkish entrepreneurs, firms, and other customers wherever they may be active is fundamental to the Ziraat Finance Group’s approach. Members of the group continue to seek out ways in which to expand the synergies among them.
