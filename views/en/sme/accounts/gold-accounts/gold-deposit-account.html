Open a Gold Account Guaranteed by Ziraat!

ShortDescription

With your Gold Deposit Account you may buy and sell gold in TL and USD. Easily accessible on demand through our extensive countrywide network of branches as well as our Internet Branch.

Page Content

Features

  • With your Gold Deposit Account you may buy and sell gold exempt from income tax and free of any charge, with a minimum transaction amount of 1 gram.
  • Gold bought and sold through these accounts is of 1000/1000 purity. Announced gold prices are prices for 1000/1000 pure gold.
  • Physical gold delivery is not available during account opening or transactions. Similarly, physical gold payments can not be made.
