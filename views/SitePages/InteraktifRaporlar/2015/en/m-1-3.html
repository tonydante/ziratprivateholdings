Financial Indicators

Ziraat Bank Shareholding Structure
The Undersecretariat of the Treasury of the Republic of Turkey is the sole owner of Ziraat Bank. The Chairman and Members of the Board of Directors, Members of the Board of Auditors, the CEO and Executive Vice Presidents do not hold shares in the Bank.
